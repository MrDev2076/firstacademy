Historic Vs. Historical

Historic is a term generally given to an important, momentous or significant event.

The term historical is a neutral word simply used to state an object is concerned or related to history.

Example:

Neil Armstrong's walk on the moon was a historic event.

Gone With the Wind is a historical novel.
